# Methodology Overview

This repository represents a research-oriented implementation
of algorithmic trading strategies for cryptocurrency markets.

The work focuses on:
- Studying trend-following and momentum-based strategies
- Evaluating robustness across BTC and ETH markets
- Applying disciplined backtesting and risk evaluation
- Maintaining reproducibility and clean data handling

The project emphasizes research structure and engineering
clarity rather than live deployment or automated trading.

Historical market data is handled programmatically to
ensure reproducibility and avoid data versioning issues.
